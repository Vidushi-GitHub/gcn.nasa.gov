**Launch Date:** June 24, 2024

**Start of Science Operations:** September 2024

**End of Operations:** Mission duration of 3 years

**Data Archive:**
[HEASARC SVOM Archive](https://heasarc.gsfc.nasa.gov/docs/heasarc/missions/svom.html)

The Space-based multi-band astronomical Variable Objects Monitor [SVOM](https://www.svom.eu/en/the-svom-mission/) is a Franco-Chinese mission, result of a collaboration between the two national space agencies, [CNSA (China National Space Administration)](https://www.cnsa.gov.cn/english/index.html) and [CNES (Centre national d'études spatiales)](https://irfu.cea.fr/dap/en/Phocea/Vie_des_labos/Ast/ast_technique.php?id_ast=2276). SVOM mission is dedicated to the study of the most distant explosions of stars, the gamma-ray bursts (GRBs).

SVOM mission consists of 4 main instruments of which 2 are French (ECLAIRs and MXT) and 2 are Chinese (GRM and VT), transient alert messages will be sent for:

#### JSON-Serialized GCN Notice Types in GCN Kafka

SVOM distributes alerts and localizations detections of gamma-ray transients. These notices are published on the GCN Kafka topic `gcn.notices.svom`.

The [GCN schema](/schema/stable/gcn/notices/svom/alert.schema.json) and
example [JSON ECLAIRs message](gcn/notices/svom/eclairs/alert.slewing.example.json) and [JSON GRM message](gcn/notices/svom/grm/alert.grm.example.json) files are available to use. See the [Schema Browser](/docs/schema/stable/gcn/notices/svom/alert.schema.json) for more information on the properties defined in the schema.

[Detailed Description and Examples](https://github.com/nasa-gcn/gcn-schema/tree/main/gcn/notices/svom)
